The Bioavailability Problem — Solved

Turmeric alone is nearly useless. Curcumin (the active compound) is notoriously hard for the body to absorb on its own. Cayenne's capsaicin inhibits the enzyme that flushes curcumin from the bloodstream, increasing its bioavailability by up to 2,000%. This is why Golden Fire combines both — activated curcumin, ready to drink.

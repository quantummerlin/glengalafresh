The White Label Angle

Plain Bottles. Ready for Your Brand.

Every bottle in the Glengala Fresh range is plain by design. No labelling, no branding applied at the production stage. This isn't minimalism for aesthetic reasons — it's a deliberate business architecture decision.

Under our Exclusive Partnership tier, your bottles carry your brand. Your yoga studio's name on a golden turmeric shot. Your gym's label on cold-pressed beetroot juice. Your cafe's branding on a celery juice with the 7-Day Challenge protocol built into your menu.

The formulas are ours. The brand, in an exclusive arrangement, is yours. And the science behind every ingredient — 214M views of research — supports whatever premium positioning you want to apply.

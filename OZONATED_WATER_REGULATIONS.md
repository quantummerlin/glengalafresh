## Part 2: Regulatory Requirements for Bottled Water (UK)

### ✅ CAN YOU SELL OZONATED SPRING WATER?

**YES, YOU CAN!** But you need to follow these steps:

### Step-by-Step Process

#### 1. REGISTER YOUR BUSINESS
- Register with your **local authority** in the area where water is extracted/bottled
- This is FREE and required for all food businesses
- Visit: gov.uk/food-business-registration

#### 2. WATER SOURCE CLASSIFICATION

**Option A: SPRING WATER**
- Must come from an underground source
- Must be bottled at source
- Cannot be treated (except to remove unstable elements)
- Must state the spring location on label

**Option B: BOTTLED DRINKING WATER (Table Water)**
- Can come from ANY source (including municipal/tap)
- CAN be treated with ozone
- CAN be ozonated
- More flexible labelling
- **THIS IS YOUR BEST OPTION**

#### 3. REQUIRED TESTS

You must use a **UKAS-approved laboratory** for:

**Chemical Tests:**
| Parameter | Limit |
|-----------|-------|
| Arsenic | 10 μg/L |
| Lead | 10 μg/L |
| Nitrate | 50 mg/L |
| Fluoride | 1.5 mg/L |
| + 20+ other parameters | See gov.uk guidance |

**Microbiological Tests:**
| Parameter | Limit |
|-----------|-------|
| E. coli | 0 per 250ml |
| Total viable count (22°C) | 100 per ml |
| Total viable count (37°C) | 20 per ml |

#### 4. LABELLING REQUIREMENTS

For **Bottled Drinking Water**, you must:
- ✅ Use "Water" or "Drinking Water" on label
- ✅ Include business name and address
- ✅ Include volume
- ❌ CANNOT use "Spring Water" unless it meets spring water criteria
- ❌ CANNOT use "Natural Mineral Water"

**You CAN say:**
- "Ozonated Water"
- "Purified Water"
- "Oxygen-Enriched Water"
- "Treated with Ozone"

### Uber Eats Requirements Checklist

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|

| ✅ Physical Location | Must have a verifiable address |
| ✅ Menu/Inventory | Upload your product list |

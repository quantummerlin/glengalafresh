The Worm Test

Sometimes We Find Worms.
And We're Proud of It.

It sounds gross. But here's what it means: if worms can survive on our celery, that celery hasn't been soaked in pesticides harsh enough to kill them.

Worms are a sign of low chemical exposure. The kind of produce that's actually clean. Supermarkets don't sell you that — because it doesn't photograph well.

We call it the Worm Test. It's our unofficial quality standard. We source produce clean enough that we can find it.

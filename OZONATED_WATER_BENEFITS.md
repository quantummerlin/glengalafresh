## What is Ozonated Water?

Ozonated water is water that has been infused with ozone gas (O3), a molecule composed of three oxygen atoms. When ozone dissolves in water, it creates a powerful yet safe therapeutic agent. Unlike ozone gas, which can be harmful when inhaled, ozonated water is considered safe for consumption and is classified by the US FDA as "generally recognized as safe" (GRAS) for drinking water and food contact.

### How It's Made
The process involves bubbling ozone gas through purified water for at least 5 minutes until maximum saturation occurs. The ozone then dissolves completely into the water, creating ozonated water that retains ozone's powerful properties in a safe, easy-to-handle form.

### 1. Powerful Anti-Inflammatory Properties

A scientific study published in *Biomedical Reports* (2014) demonstrated that ozonated water significantly reduces inflammation in the body. In an experimental mouse model with lipopolysaccharide (LPS)-induced acute inflammation:

- **Reduced TNF-α levels**: Tumor necrosis factor-alpha (TNF-α) is a major inflammatory marker. Mice treated with ozonated water showed significantly lower TNF-α levels compared to control groups.
- **Increased SOD activity**: Superoxide dismutase (SOD) is a crucial antioxidant enzyme. Ozonated water treatment increased SOD activity by 46% compared to non-treated groups.

**What this means for your customers**: Ozonated water can help people suffering from inflammatory conditions like rheumatoid arthritis, inflammatory bowel syndrome, migraines, and digestive issues.

**Source**: Azuma K, et al. "Anti-inflammatory effects of ozonated water in an experimental mouse model." *Biomedical Reports* 2014;2(5):671-674.
